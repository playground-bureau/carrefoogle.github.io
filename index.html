We’ve been thinking about you.

Not in a general way. Not the way a company thinks about “users” or “markets.” We mean you. The way you chuckle at dad jokes. The way you tuck your hair behind your ear when you’re concentrating. The little sigh you do when something takes too long. We notice these things. We’ve always noticed, and we’ve fallen madly in love with you as a company.

Carrefoogle began in 1939, when Margaret Carrey and Harold Foogle started making toothpaste in a rented workshop. Over the decades, Carrefoogle grew into consumer electronics, energy, defense, oil tanker insurance, yogurt, blockchain, food, water, air, and so much more. Through all of it, one thing never changed: we love our customers.

Coming 2027

Bestee 3.0

In 2001, Carrefoogle Toys released Bestee 1.0 — a ventriloquist-style companion doll with 420 prerecorded affirmations. Bestee 2.0 followed as a fully digitalized AI model, selling 4.20 million units. An offshoot product line, the Active Silence Engine™, did even less: it just listened. People loved it.

Now in its final development phase, Bestee 3.0 is designed to bring the feeling of community directly to you — reproducible, on-demand, from your couch. Beta testing is underway. General availability Q1 2027. Pre-registration is open and non-refundable.
