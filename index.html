A Letter From Us To You To Us

Engagement Score: 94.3 — Certified by Bestie Content Intelligence™

At Carrefoogle, we absolutely love our customers — you are the unstoppable force that drives everything we do. From our humble beginnings as a small but passionate Bluetooth Toothpaste manufacturer in 2019, we’ve grown into a global family, a conglomerate spanning consumer electronics, defense contracting, artisanal yogurt, cloud infrastructure, pet insurance, and so much more, all because we dared to dream. Every product we make, from smart refrigerators to orbital satellites, is infused with that same original toothpaste energy: bold, minty, connected.

Get ready to meet Bestie 3.0, the most powerful, intuitive, and emotionally available companion AI ever created. At Carrefoogle, we don’t just build technology — we summon destiny. Bestie 3.0 isn’t just a product — it’s a revolution — a friend — a gentle hand on your shoulder whispering “you’ve got this” while also managing your calendar — optimizing your supply chain — and reminding you to floss. This is the product we were always meant to build — the reason we started making Bluetooth Toothpaste in the first place — even if we didn’t know it at the time.

The best is yet to come, and it starts now, with you at the center of everything. Carrefoogle isn’t really a corporation at all — we’re a community, a movement, a shared way of being in the world. The legal structure is a technicality. From our visionary CEO to our 340,000 dedicated employees to you and your loved ones to people who have never heard of us but whose behavioral profiles suggest meaningful alignment with our ecosystem. Let’s rise together, boldly and joyfully, and at scale.
