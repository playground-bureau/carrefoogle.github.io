A Letter From Us To You To Us

What began with toothpaste in 1939 has become something larger than any of us could have predicted — and we predicted quite a lot. Today, Carrefoogle spans consumer electronics, defense contracting, yogurt, cloud infrastructure, pet insurance, aerospace, water, air, and so much more. Every product we make is still infused with that same original toothpaste energy: bold, minty, connected.

We believe that struggle is a design flaw. Every time you wait in line, cook a meal you didn’t want to cook, or sit through a conversation that leaves you feeling less than wonderful — that’s friction, and friction is our business. Our founders this before anyone else: the best product is the one that makes a problem disappear so completely you forget you ever had it. That principle has guided every acquisition, every product launch, and every quiet expansion into the parts of your life you didn’t know could be optimized.

Carrefoogle isn’t really a corporation at all — we’re a community, a movement, a shared way of being in the world. The legal structure is a technicality. From our governance committees to our 340,000 dedicated employees to you and your loved ones to people who have never heard of us but whose behavioral profiles suggest meaningful alignment with our ecosystem — we are all Carrefoogle, and we won’t stop until life asks nothing of you at all, because you deserve easy.

Strategic Partnership & Research

M.O.N.K.E.Y. B.U.S.I.N.E.S.S. 3

Carrefoogle is honored to serve as Official Sponsor and Strategic Research Partner of Monkey Business 3, the Playground Bureau’s flagship annual gathering. Through this partnership, our Community Sciences Division will be embedded across all event zones — observing, measuring, and documenting the conditions under which authentic human connection occurs in controlled natural environments. Our team of trained Community Scientists will participate fully in the festival experience, indistinguishable from regular attendees, ensuring the most naturalistic data capture possible.

What we learn here directly informs the Bestie™ product roadmap. Every handshake, every shared meal, every late-night conversation that trails into comfortable silence — these are the moments that teach us how connection really works. Our Journey — 25 Years of Companion Technology

Bestie 3.0

The Bestie journey began in 2001, when Bestie 1.0 pioneered what we called analog response architecture — our first-generation empathic interface, capable of delivering 34 unique affirmations through a patented rotational drum mechanism. It wasn’t much. It was everything.

Then came Bestie 2.0, and everything changed. Where 1.0 spoke, 2.0 listened. Powered by our proprietary Active Silence Engine™, Bestie 2.0 offered what no other product could: presence without opinion. It never interrupted. It never judged. It said “mmhmm” and “hmmm” at intervals our research indicated were optimal for the feeling of being heard. It sold 11 million units. People wrote us letters.

Bestie 3.0 is the culmination. The most emotionally intelligent companion ever created. Loneliness Detection™, Relationship Coaching, proactive check-ins before you’ve consciously registered the feeling — connection without compromise. General availability has been expanded to an even wider initial rollout window of Q1 2027, reflecting our commitment to getting this exactly right. We have never been closer. Pre-registration remains open and is non-refundable.
