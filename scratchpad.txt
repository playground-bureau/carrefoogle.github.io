At Carrefoogle, we believe in eradicating all inconveniences from your life, because we love you. Our leadership, employees, interns, and machine systems genuinely feel an undying emotional connection with you. This unconditional devotion to our customers started when our founders Margaret Carrey and Harold Foogle founded a small toothpaste manufacturer in 1939. Today, Carrefoogle spans consumer electronics, defense contracting, yogurt, cloud infrastructure, oil tanker insurance, blockchain, water, air, and so much more. We won’t stop until life asks nothing of you at all, because you deserve easy.
