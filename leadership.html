Harold Foogle & Margaret Carrey
Our Founders

At Carrefoogle, no single individual is responsible for any one decision. Our governance model distributes leadership across interlocking bodies of equal standing, designed for resilience, continuity, and the principled diffusion of responsibility. No body listed below holds authority over any other. Below is a current directory of our leadership structure.

This directory reflects governance appointments as of March 2026. Carrefoogle’s leadership structure is reviewed quarterly by the Governance Renewal Subcommittee, which reports to the Ethics & Values Alignment Board, which reports to the Board of Stewardship, which reports to the full governance body. All bodies hold equal standing within the governance framework. Individuals listed may serve on multiple bodies concurrently. No single listing implies decision-making authority. For governance inquiries, please contact your regional Governance Liaison, or submit a request through the Inter-Divisional Synergy Coordination Office, which will route it to the appropriate body within 6–8 weeks.
