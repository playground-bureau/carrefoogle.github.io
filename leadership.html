At Carrefoogle, no single individual is responsible for any one decision. Our governance model distributes leadership across interlocking bodies of equal standing, designed for resilience, continuity, and the principled diffusion of responsibility. No body listed below holds authority over any other.

Since 2024, our governance framework has been maintained and periodically refined using proprietary alignment tools to ensure maximum structural resilience.
