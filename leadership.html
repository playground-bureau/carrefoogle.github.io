Leadership

Harold Foogle & Margaret Carrey
Our Founders

At Carrefoogle, we believe the fastest path to a decision is one where no single individual can be blamed for it. Leadership is distributed across 147 interlocking bodies of equal standing so that decisions can be made quickly, confidently, and by no one in particular.

Coordination between bodies is handled by Bestee™, which takes care of the small things—drafting agendas, circulating recommendations, implementing decisions, and determining which decisions require approval—so our human leaders can focus on what matters most: focusing on leadership.
